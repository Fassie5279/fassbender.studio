Brushing up my vector skills 

It has always been a dream of mine to be able to create any logo or complete brand identity I want in a vector program. I've been dabbling and playing around on an off with different apps like Illustrator, Inkscape and now, Affinity. I have a lot of ideas in my head for designs and I just want to build those best I can. In order for me to brush up my vector skills, I'm now taking "How to Make Logos in Affinity Designer", by Affinity Revolution. 

Even though the course may look "simple" and is aimed at beginners, that's exactly what I need now! I will be logging my progress in the course here. Stay tuned!
